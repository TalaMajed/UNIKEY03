UJ's Yearbook 2024-2025 Has Been Launched

We are excited to announce that the UJ's Yearbook for the academic year 2024-2025 has been officially launched. This year's edition features a comprehensive look at the achievements, events, and memorable moments of our university community.

Highlights of this year's yearbook include:

  • - Exclusive interviews with faculty and students
  • - Photo galleries from major university events
  • - Special sections dedicated to graduating students

Copies of the yearbook are available for purchase at the university bookstore. Don't miss out on this keepsake that captures the spirit of our university!
